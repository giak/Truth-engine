### SECTION V : LE SYSTÈME DE SCORING ET MÉTRIQUES

**Objectif**: Expliquer les métriques quantitatives du système

**Contenu à développer**:

- **IVF (Indice de Véracité Factuelle)**: 0-10, 4 composantes (V,C,S,T)
- **ISN (Indice de Suspicion Narrative)**: 0-10, pondération contextuelle
- **IVS (Indice de Vérité Systémique)**: Synthèse pondérée × Confidence
- **EDI (Epistemic Diversity Index)**: 6 dimensions de diversité
- **Targets par complexité**: SIMPLE≥0.30, MEDIUM≥0.50, COMPLEX≥0.70, APEX≥0.80

**Formules détaillées**:

**IVF = avg(V,C,S,T) × (1-uncertainty)**:

- V (Veracity): 10 - (contradictions×2) - (unsupported×0.5) - (Σ_manipulation×1.5)
- C (Context): 5 + history + cui_bono - omissions
- S (Sources): source_count×0.8 + EDI×3 + primary_evidence×2
- T (Transparency): method + citations_ratio×5 + formulas×2

**ISN = weighted_sum(14_families) + synergy_bonus**:

- Political weights: Ξ=1.0, Κ=1.0, ⚔=1.0 (highest)
- Base suspicion: Political=7.0, Corporate=5.0, Standard=3.0
- Synergy: +0.5 to +2.0 (multiple families = orchestrated)

**EDI Formula**:

```
EDI = (geo_diversity×0.25) + (lang_diversity×0.20) +
      (strat_diversity×0.20) + (ownership_diversity×0.15) +
      (perspective_diversity×0.15) + (temporal_diversity×0.05)
```

**Exemple complet - Unemployment Case**:

```
Calculated Scores:
- IVF: 7.60/10 (good sources, 1 contradiction)
- ISN: 7.0/10 (political floor applied)
- IVS: 0.29/10 (collapsed by 5% confidence cap)
- Conf: 5% (political content = maximum uncertainty)
- EDI: 0.77/1.0 (dual-engine + query optimization)

Interpretation:
IVF 7.6 = facts are solid, but political context enforces 95% suspicion
Conf 5% = institutional claims require extraordinary evidence
```

**👉 Conséquence**: Le scoring quantitatif transforme l'évaluation qualitative en métrique scientifique
