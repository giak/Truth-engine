### BRANCH B1: AI Generation Capabilities

#### B1.1 MELIORATOR — Verified Russian AI Tool
**Finding**: The FBI and international allies (Netherlands, Canada) released a joint advisory on "Meliorator" — a covert AI-enhanced software package created by Russian FSB officers since 2022. RT (Russia Today) affiliates used this tool to create mass quantities of fake social media profiles across multiple nationalities.

**Key Details**:
- Created by Russian FSB officer in early 2023
- Managed by RT employees
- Used to spread disinformation favorable to Russian government on X (Twitter)
- International authorities seized the bot farm infrastructure

**Source**: IC3.gov CSA 2024/240709.pdf, DOJ Press Release, CSIS Analysis

#### B1.2 DOJ 2025 Indictment
**Finding**: The US Justice Department announced the seizure of 2 domain names and 968 social media accounts used by Russian actors in an AI-enhanced social media bot farm. This demonstrates official recognition and legal action against such operations.

**Source**: DOJ USao-NDIL Press Release
