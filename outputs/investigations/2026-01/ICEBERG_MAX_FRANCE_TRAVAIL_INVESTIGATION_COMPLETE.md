## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

France Travail (formerly Pôle emploi) suffered a historic data breach affecting **43 million French citizens** (over 2/3 of the population) between February 6 and March 5, 2024. The attack went undetected for **28 days**. On January 29, 2026, CNIL fined France Travail **€5 million** for manifestly inadequate security measures.

This investigation reveals a **systemic institutional failure pattern** with circular citizen punishment logic: citizens pay taxes → state fails to secure data → citizens' data stolen → citizens pay for remediation via taxes → no individual accountability → cycle repeats.

## KEY FINDINGS SUMMARY

### The Shadoks Logic (Systemic Dysfunction)

The France Travail case exemplifies what the user called **"Shadocks logic"** - a system that pumps (pompe) without producing results, while extracting value:

1. **Underinvestment → Breach** (citizens pay for cheap security)
2. **Breach → Fine** (citizens pay for the fine)
3. **Fine → Remediation** (citizens pay for remediation)
4. **Remediation → No improvement** (cycle repeats)
5. **Political accountability → Zero** (no resignations, no prosecutions)
