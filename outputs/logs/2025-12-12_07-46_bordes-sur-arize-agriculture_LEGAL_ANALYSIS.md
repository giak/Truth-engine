## 📜 CONSTITUTIONAL FRAMEWORK ANALYSIS

### FRENCH CONSTITUTIONAL PROTECTION - ARTICLE 544 CIVIL CODE

**LEGAL FOUNDATION**:

> **Article 544 du Code Civil français**: _"Le droit de propriété est le droit de jouir et disposer des choses de la manière la plus absolue pourvu qu'on n'en fasse pas un usage prohibé par la loi ou les règlements."_

**TRANSLATION**: _"Property right is the right to enjoy and dispose of things in the most absolute way, provided that one does not make use of it prohibited by law or regulations."_

**CONSTITUTIONAL IMPLICATIONS**:
✅ **Fundamental Right**: Absolute property rights are constitutionally protected
✅ **Limited Exceptions**: Only when specifically "prohibited by law or regulations"
✅ **Strict Interpretation**: Exceptions to property rights must be explicitly provided
