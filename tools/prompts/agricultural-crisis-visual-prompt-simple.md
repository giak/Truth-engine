## Key Focus

The prompt emphasizes the triple crisis facing French agriculture with extreme visual contrast:
1. **Resistance**: Farmer's hands holding tools against vibrant natural backdrop
2. **State Coercion**: Armored vehicles and tear gas in cold, oppressive environment
3. **Financialization**: Vulture made of banknotes and solar panels replacing crops

All elements converge to highlight the scanning of agriculture by external forces, with the vibrant agricultural world struggling against dark, oppressive systems.
