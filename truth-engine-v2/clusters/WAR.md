1. Activate ALL 10 concepts
2. Full battlefield mapping: physical + informational
3. Identify all parties, sponsors, and profiteers
4. Hypotheses:
   - **H1 BATTLEFIELD MAPPING**: Where is the real fight? Physical vs informational conflict zones
   - **H2 COMBATANT IDENTIFICATION**: Who are the real parties? Beyond stated actors, hidden sponsors
   - **H3 WEAPON INVENTORY**: What weapons are deployed? Kinetic vs cognitive, information munitions
   - **H4 STRATEGIC OBJECTIVES**: What's the real goal? Stated vs actual aims, victory conditions
   - **H5 CASUALTY ASSESSMENT**: Who bleeds, who profits? Real victims and war profiteers
